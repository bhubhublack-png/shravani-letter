A Letter From the Heart

For Shravani, with love

My dearest Shravani,

01I am writing this with a heavy heart and trembling hands, because some things need to be said.

02I am sorry. Truly, deeply, completely sorry — and I mean every syllable of that.

03I mistreated you, and there is no excuse worthy of that truth.

04You deserved kindness in every moment, and I gave you something far less.

05You deserved to feel safe with me, and I failed to build that safety for you.

06When you needed me most, I wasn't there — and that haunts me every single day.

07You reached out for my hand, and I let the distance swallow us whole.

08I was selfish when I should have been selfless, silent when I should have spoken.

09You are someone who gives everything — your time, your warmth, your whole soul.

10And I took that for granted, as if your love were a given and not a gift.

11I should have shown up — not just in body, but in spirit, in attention, in care.

12I should have asked how you were, not just assumed that you were fine.

13Every time you smiled and said "it's okay," I wish I had looked closer.

14Because it wasn't always okay, and I was too wrapped up to notice.

15I am sorry for the words I said when I should have stayed quiet.

16I am sorry for the words I didn't say when they would have meant the world.

17I am sorry for making you feel small in any moment — you are anything but small.

18You are enormous — enormous in heart, in courage, in the way you love people.

19You carry so much without complaint, and I added to your weight instead of lifting it.

20That was wrong. And I own it completely.

21I don't deserve forgiveness, but I am asking for it anyway — humbly, honestly.

22Not because it erases the past, but because I want to do better for your future.

23You are the kind of person who makes the world brighter just by existing in it.

24Your laughter is a sound I never want to stop hearing.

25Your strength — especially now, on the eve of the most important exam of your life —

26is the most inspiring thing I have ever witnessed up close.

27You studied when it was hard. You pushed when you were tired.

28You kept going when the world felt heavy — including the weight I added to it.

29That tells me everything about who you are: extraordinary.

30I should have been your anchor. I should have been your peace.

31From here on, that is exactly what I intend to be.

32I promise to show up — really show up — when you need someone beside you.

33I promise to listen more, assume less, and love louder.

34I promise that your feelings will never be dismissed or minimised again.

35I promise that the version of me you deserve is the one I am choosing to become.

36You asked me to apologise, and I hope this letter gives you a piece of my heart.

37Not the polished, rehearsed kind — the raw, real, vulnerable kind.

38Because that is what you deserve: honesty, not performance.

39And above all else, I want you to know — you are loved. Completely.

40Go into tomorrow's exam knowing that someone in your corner believes in you fiercely.

41Every page you studied, every night you stayed up — it was all worth it.

42Walk into that hall with your head high, Shravani. You are ready.

43And when you come out the other side, I will be there — this time, I promise.

44All my love, all my sorrys, all my tomorrows — they are yours. 🌸

Yours, always —
✨
